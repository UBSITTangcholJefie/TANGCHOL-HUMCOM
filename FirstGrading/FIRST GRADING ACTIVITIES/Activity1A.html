Grilled Liempo with Barbeque Sauce

By: Vanjo Merano

https://panlasangpinoy.com/grilled-liempo-with-barbecue-sauce/

Description

This dish needs no introduction at all because the name of the dish already introduces itself. It is also popular and favorite dish in the Philippines and you can find this dish at any restaurant that claims it serves Filipino cuisine. This dish finds itself to be a constant presence, be it at children’s parties, family gatherings, or an inuman session with friends. This kind of dish is prepared in a simple manner and the cooking process is not complicated, but it is full of awesome flavor. Grilled liempo truly is one of the best meat dishes to enjoy, whether it’s by yourself or with family. Don’t be afraid to try it today!

Ingredients
  • 1 lb. pork belly sliced

Marinade Ingredients
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce
  • 5 tablespoons knorr liquid seasoning
  • 3 pieces calamansi
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Equipment
  • Grill

Instructions
  1. Combine marinade ingredients in a large bowl. Stir until well blended.
  2. Add pork belly. Make sure that the pork gets coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl and marinate for at least 3 hours. Note: the longer the time, the more flavorful the mixture gets.
  3. Heat-up the grill. Grill one side of the pork for 2 minutes. Baste the top part by brushing the remaining marinade. Turn the pork belly over and do the same procedure on the opposite side. Note: Continue this step until the pork is cooked completely.
  4. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with warm rice along with your favorite condiments. Share and enjoy!
